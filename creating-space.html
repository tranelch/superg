Creating Space

Creating Space is a personal organizing business started by Ann Corazon Ganch in 2008. It sprung into existence as a common-sense antidote to the American dilemma of "too much!" Ann works with individuals in areas of their home, office and/or grounds that feel overwhelming or stressful. Together, we discuss your space and create a focused plan on how to move forward. The goal is not to get rid of everything, but rather to know what you have and how to find it. What is no longer needed is passed on or recycled. Working together in good cheer makes the psychological hurdles easier, and gets the job done in half the time. As we clear and clean outwardly, space is simultaneously created in the inner landscapes of our minds, allowing us to reclaim Home as a place of rejuvenation and inspiration.
